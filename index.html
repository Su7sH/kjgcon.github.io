About the College

Rooted in community, built for the future.

Kasabai Janardan Gunjal College of Nursing is an institution of the Om Chaitanya Foundation, established to bring structured, modern nursing education to Alephata and the surrounding region.

Students learn alongside a working multi-specialty hospital, gaining real clinical exposure from the very first year. Our mission is simple: prepare nurses who can serve both the rural clinic and the modern hospital with equal confidence and care.
